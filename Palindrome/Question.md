# Palindrome

## Objective:

- The goal is to check whether the given number is a palindrome or not.
 
## Example:
- 1 input: 121     output: palindrome
- 2 input: 3024    output: not a palindrome

## Constraints:

- The number is a integer and does not exceed 10000.
- output is a string.
